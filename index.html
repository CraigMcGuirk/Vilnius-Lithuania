Old Town

Pilies Street

Pilies Street is the Old Town's main artery and the hub of cafe and street market life. It is a rather short street, running from Cathedral Square to the Town Hall Square.

Užupis

Užupis is a neighborhood in Vilnius, largely located in Vilnius's old town. Užupis means "beyond the river" or "the other side of the river" in the Lithuanian language and refers to the Vilnia River

Vilnius University

Vilnius University Architectural Ensemble, occupies a large part of the Old Town and has 13 courtyards.

UNESCO World Heritage Site

In 1994 the Vilnius Old Town was included as a UNESCO World Heritage Site (No. 541) in recognition of its universal value and originality.
